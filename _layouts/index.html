उत्तिष्ठत जाग्रत प्राप्य वरान्निबोधत ।
क्षुरस्य धारा निशिता दुरत्यया दुर्गं पथस्तत्कवयो वदन्ति ॥ १४ ॥

Arise, awake; having reached the great, learn;
Like the sharp edge of a razor is that path, so the wise say−hard to tread and difficult to cross.

     ~ KathaUpanishad(Verse 1.3.14)
